Everick Foundation Annual Report

Everick Foundation's mission is to create pathways for Indigenous communities to engage with culture and heritage on Country. We facilitate community-led initiatives in capacity building, education, research and creative solutions. We foster collaborations to support community aspirations, shared understandings, and visual storytelling about heritage.
