Western Kangoulu - Welcome to Country Signage

Design Concept - To display Western Kangaulu's cultural image to miners at the entrance of several Kestrel Mines as a welcome to country for incoming staff. This would utilise printed stickers to be placed on the Kestrel mine entryway. 

Design Matthew Haynes

Client  Western Kangoulu / Kestel Coal
