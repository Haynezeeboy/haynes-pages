Western Kangoulu - Welcome to Country Signage

Design Concept - To represent Western Kangaulu's cultural image to miners at the Kestrel Mine and welcome workers on their land. This would utilise printed stickers to be placed on the Kestrel mine entryway. 

Design Matthew Haynes

Client  Western Kangoulu / Kestel Coal
